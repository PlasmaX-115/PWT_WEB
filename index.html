Led by PAS, this is a game that revolves around percussion instruments around the globe. From America to Asia, going through Africa, our main goal is to show the players the diversity in cultures and their music; as their drums are strongly tied to their history. We aim to achieve all of this with a simple yet fun musical game that will test the players skills and challenging them to catch the rhythm.

Next to PAS, we manage to gather data about players around the globe in order to analyze and undestand how users like or dislike the game, so as how their abilities increase as they continue playings. To check this data, click on the following button. Tableau
